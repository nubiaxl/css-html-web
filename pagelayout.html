Page Layout

Good
  • Appealing to target audience
  • Consistent site header/logo
  • Consistent navigation area
  • Informative page title that includes the company/organization/site name
  • Good use of basic design principles: repetition, contrast, proximity, alignment
  • Balance of text/graphics/white space on page
  • Good contrast between text and background
  • Home page has compelling, interesting information above the fold at 800x600 resolution
  • Home page downloads within 10 seconds on dial-up connection
Bad
  • Page footer area -- copyright, last update, contact e-mail address
  • Displays without horizontal scrolling at 800x600 and higher resolutions
  • Repetitive information (header/logo and navigation) takes up no more than one-quarter to one-third of the browser window at 800x600 resolution
